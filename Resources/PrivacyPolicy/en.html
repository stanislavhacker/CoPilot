Privacy policy

Privacy policy: Location service

CoPilot application uses location services to determine the position of the device. Location is stored in the application and used for subsequent display on the map. Location is not sent outside the application and everything is always stored in the internal memory. Location is not provided to third parties and use it to CoPilot application only for its internal purposes. The function of location services, you can directly turn off from application.

Privacy policy: Bluetooth

Application CoPilot uses bluetooth to connect OBD devices that allow you to read data from the control unit of the car. With that saves the state of car at the time and it is possible to scroll back through the state car during the ride. These data are not sent anywhere and remain only in internal application memory. Bluetooth function, you can directly turn off from application.
